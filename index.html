Holy Speech!

a speech memorization tool

1. Using the "Add to speech" button, add the paragraphs of your speech one by one. Each paragraph must be accompanied by a brief ~10 word description.
2. Save the text in the row below somewhere so you can use your speech again in the future.
3. Click the "Load speech" button and read through your speech.
4. When you're done, press the "Remove some words" button to, you guessed it, replace some of the words in your speech with blanks.
5. Try and fill in the blanks as you read your speech out loud. Repeat steps 4-5 as many times as it takes.
6. Refresh the page to change speeches.
